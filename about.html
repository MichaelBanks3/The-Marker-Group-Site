About

Built on real relationships and real results in higher education.

The Marker Group was founded to help education technology companies succeed in one of the most complex and rewarding markets in enterprise software.

Two decades of selling into higher ed — from the front lines to the boardroom.

The Marker Group was founded by a veteran sales leader who spent 20+ years building, leading, and scaling SaaS sales organizations in the higher education market — primarily with Workday, one of the most consequential technology platforms in the space.

From carrying a bag as an individual contributor to leading high-performing sales teams, he's navigated every stage of the sales lifecycle: territory development, executive engagement, complex procurement cycles, and multi-year expansion strategies across research universities, state systems, and private institutions.

That experience — the hard-won pattern recognition, the institutional relationships, the understanding of what actually moves deals forward in higher ed — is what The Marker Group brings to every engagement.
